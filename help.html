Getting Started

How to Sign Up

  1. Visit Lexorium

    Go to lexoriumai.com and click "Start for free" or "Sign in to Lexorium".

  2. Create Your Account

    Sign in with Google, GitHub, or email to access Lexorium instantly.

  3. Start Asking Questions

    Type your legal or compliance question in the chat box and press Enter or click Send.

Tip: Indian Law Default

Lexorium defaults to Indian law when no jurisdiction is specified. You can ask about any jurisdiction in your question.

Structured Responses

Every answer includes Issue, Applicable Law, Analysis, Conclusion, Practical Note, and Disclaimer.

Get quick responses to everyday legal questions with priority processing on Pro plans.

Legal Workflows

Contract Drafting

Click the Attach button and select "Contract Drafting" to prepare legal drafts based on your requirements. Works best with Pro plans.

Document Summarisation

Upload legal documents and select "Summarise" mode to get structured summaries highlighting key clauses and legal implications.

Document Analysis

Upload contracts, NDAs, agreements, or any legal document for detailed analysis. Lexorium will identify issues, risks, and recommendations.

Research Tool

Use the Research mode for comprehensive legal research on specific topics, case precedents, or statutory provisions.

Predictive Risk Scoring

Available on Pro plans. Get risk scores from 1-100 with clear risk bands (Low, Moderate, High, Severe) and the legal drivers behind the assessment.

How to Access Workflows

Click the "+" button (Legal Tools) next to the chat input to see all available workflows. Some features require Pro or Enterprise plans.

Attaching Documents

Supported Formats

You can upload images (JPG, PNG, WebP), PDFs, Word documents (DOCX), text files, and CSVs for analysis.

How to Attach

  1. Click the Attach Button

    Look for the "+" button or paperclip icon near the chat input box.

  2. Choose Your File

    Select "Photo" or "File/PDF" from the menu. On mobile, you can also use "Camera" to take a photo directly.

  3. Ask Your Question

    Type your question about the document and press Send. Lexorium will analyze the attachment.

Taking Photos

On mobile devices, use the Camera option to take photos of documents, IDs, or contracts for instant analysis.

Voice Features

Voice Input (Pro & Enterprise)

Tap the microphone button next to the chat box to speak your legal question. Lexorium will transcribe and answer it.

Voice Analysis (Pro & Enterprise)

Use the Voice Analysis feature from the Legal Tools menu to describe your legal situation by voice for detailed analysis.

Answer Playback

Click the play button on any Lexorium response to hear it read aloud. Useful for review or accessibility.

Voice Settings

Customize voice preferences in Settings > Voice. You can choose between Google preferred or System default voices.

Customizing Your Experience

Response Style

In Settings, adjust the base tone, warmth, enthusiasm, and formatting to match your preferences while maintaining legal accuracy.

Persona Selection

Choose your Lexorium persona in Settings to customize how responses are framed (e.g., as a lawyer, compliance officer, or general advisor).

Primary Use Case

Set your primary use case to help Lexorium tailor responses to your specific legal needs.

Model Selection

Pro and Enterprise users can choose between different AI models based on speed vs. depth requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lexorium a substitute for a lawyer?

No. Lexorium provides legal information to help you understand legal concepts. It is not legal advice and should not replace consultation with a qualified lawyer.

What jurisdiction does Lexorium cover?

Lexorium defaults to Indian law but can answer questions about any jurisdiction. Specify the jurisdiction in your question for accurate results.

How accurate are the legal answers?

Lexorium provides structured, well-reasoned responses based on legal principles. Always verify critical information with primary sources or legal counsel.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your conversations are encrypted and stored securely. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing.

Can I export my conversations?

Yes, on Pro and Enterprise plans. Use the export option in the chat menu to download your conversation history.

What happens when I hit my daily limit?

You'll see a limit message. Upgrade to Pro for more queries or wait until midnight (IST) for your daily reset.
