Privacy Policy

Privacy that matches a professional legal workflow.

This Privacy Policy explains how Lexorium collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you access the website, the chat experience, billing flows, and any connected desktop application.

Information We Collect

We may collect account details, sign-in identifiers, name, email address, profile image, conversation content, uploaded files, payment metadata, usage events, device information, IP-related security data, and cookie preferences when you use Lexorium.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate your account and keep your session active.
  • To provide legal AI answers, saved history, uploads, exports, and workspace features.
  • To enforce plan limits, investigate misuse, and manage security or fraud risk.
  • To process upgrades, verify billing events, and maintain subscription status.
  • To improve product quality, retention, reliability, and support.

AI Processing and Providers

Conversation content may be processed by third-party model, infrastructure, authentication, analytics, or payment providers to operate the product. We aim to limit provider access to what is reasonably necessary for service delivery and security.

Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with approved service providers, advisors, or authorities when necessary to operate the service, fulfil legal obligations, protect rights, or investigate abuse. Cross-border processing may occur where providers operate globally.

Retention and Deletion

We retain account, billing, analytics, and conversation records for as long as reasonably necessary to operate Lexorium, honour legal obligations, resolve disputes, and prevent fraud. You may request review or deletion by contacting aisprezzatura@gmail.com.

Cookies and Analytics

Essential cookies support sign-in, security, and core site functionality. Optional analytics cookies help improve product performance and user experience where you choose to allow them.

Security and Sensitive Matters

We use authenticated sessions, backend payment verification, access restrictions, and environment-based secret handling. No system is perfectly secure, so you should avoid uploading unnecessary highly sensitive or privileged material unless you have assessed the risk for your matter.

Your Choices

You may sign out, stop using the service, request support, or contact us regarding access, correction, deletion, or other rights that may apply under law. If you do not agree with this policy, you should not use Lexorium.
